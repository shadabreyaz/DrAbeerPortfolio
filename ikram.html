A Leader in Health Transformation

I Dr. Abeer Al Teneiji, am a distinguished Emirati physician and an influential leader in the healthcare sector, committed to elevating public health and clinical excellence across the UAE and beyond. I have over 21 years of experience in healthcare and more 14 years in senior leadership roles, I have spearheaded major strategies and transformation initiatives in both government and private sectors.

As the Executive Director of Clinical Excellence at Cleveland Clinic Abu Dhabi, I focused on integrating cost-efficient methodologies with sustainable, high-quality patient services.

Key Focus Areas: Public Health Medicine, Health Care Transformation, Digital Health Innovation, Lifestyle Medicine, and Women Leadership Development.

Key Distinction: I hold an Honorary Membership with the Royal College of Physicians of the United Kingdom and I am the first UAE national physician certified in Lifestyle Medicine. I am also a founder of the Emirates Lifestyle and Longevity Medicine Society (ELLMS).
